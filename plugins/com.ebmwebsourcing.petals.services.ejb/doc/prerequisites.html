Pre-requisites

The Petals EJB plug-in uses Apache CXF to generate a WSDL at the end of the wizard.

The use of CXF is made through the WSDL Tools plug-in, available within the same update site than the Petals plug-ins. Therefore, you have to download Apache CXF and unzip it somewhere on your hard-drive. Then, specify the CXF location in the WSDL Tools preferences. It can be done by selecting Window > Preferences > Petals > WSDL Tools.
